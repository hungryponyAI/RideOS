# Users

Target users and personas for the RideOS app.

---

## Primary user: the builder (partydj)
- Owns a Wahoo KICKR Core + Zwift Cog + Zwift Click
- Wants full control over resistance logic without relying on Zwift/Rouvy
- Rides with GPX routes from Komoot
- Values feeling of real virtual gearing over raw accuracy

## Future users (post-MVP)
- Indoor cyclists who want more control than Zwift offers
- Athletes who follow structured GPX-based training
- Cyclists who want an open, hackable training platform
