04 · Ride Experience

The road is the interface.

The live ride screen is built around a 70/30 hierarchy: route first, interface second. Metrics appear only where they support riding.

Ride States

Normal: balanced density.
Climb: elevation expands, ghost becomes clearer.
Recovery: visuals calm down.

Ghost UX

Ghost pacing is personal and subtle. No racing-game avatars, no exaggerated effects, no loud victory language.

06 · Logo System

Route, ascent, continuity.
