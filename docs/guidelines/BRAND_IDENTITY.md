# OUDENA — Brand Identity

Version: 2.0

# 1. Brand Vision

OUDENA is a premium indoor cycling operating system built around:
- real-world rides
- personal cycling history
- reflective performance

The brand exists to make indoor cycling feel:
- immersive
- calm
- personal
- meaningful

---

# 2. Positioning

## Positioning Statement

“Indoor cycling built around your real-world riding history.”

---

## Core Value Proposition

Ride and improve on your own real-world routes indoors.

---

# 3. Brand Archetypes

## Primary — The Guide

Traits:
- intelligent
- calm
- capable
- supportive

## Secondary — The Explorer

Traits:
- terrain
- movement
- discovery
- memory

---

# 4. Brand Personality

OUDENA should feel:
- calm
- focused
- technical
- premium
- minimalist
- reflective
- intelligent

Never:
- loud
- childish
- aggressive
- chaotic
- esports-oriented

---

# 5. Emotional Territory

| Competitor | Emotion |
|---|---|
| Zwift | stimulation |
| TrainerRoad | discipline |
| Rouvy | realism |
| OUDENA | reflective performance |

---

# 6. Brand Philosophy

## Calm First
Reduce mental noise.

## Personal History Is Core
The rider’s own routes create emotional connection.

## Quiet Confidence
Performance is acknowledged subtly.

## Adult-Focused Experience
The product should feel mature and refined.

---

# 7. Tone of Voice

Characteristics:
- calm
- concise
- reflective
- precise
- intelligent

---

## Good Examples

- “Your pacing improved on sustained climbs.”
- “You rode more consistently today.”
- “This route matches your current endurance.”

---

## Avoid

- “CRUSH THIS CLIMB!”
- “DESTROY YOUR PR!”
- “LEVEL UP!”

---

# 8. Messaging Strategy

Focus on:
- personal history
- route familiarity
- elegant performance
- calm immersion

Avoid:
- hype marketing
- esports language
- gym culture

---

# 9. Brand Keywords

Primary:
- refined
- immersive
- calm
- focused
- premium

Secondary:
- terrain
- rhythm
- endurance
- movement

---

# 10. Product Identity

OUDENA is NOT:
- a game
- a social feed
- an esports environment

OUDENA IS:
- a cycling operating system
- a premium riding environment
- a reflective performance companion

## Older Hobby Cyclists

# 13. Onboarding Tone

Should feel:
- welcoming
- effortless
- calm

Good:
- “Import your rides.”
- “Connect your trainer.”
- “Ride familiar roads indoors.”

---

# 14. Subscription Philosophy

Premium should feel:
- additive
- respectful
- valuable

Never:
- manipulative
- restrictive

---

# 15. Brand Atmosphere

The atmosphere should feel like:
- early morning rides
- foggy climbs
- quiet endurance
- empty alpine roads

More:
- Porsche campaign

Less:
- Red Bull campaign

---

# 16. Long-Term Brand Direction

Potential future areas:

- outdoor integration

Without losing:
- calmness
- simplicity
